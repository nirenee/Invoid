# Metal-Space
Videogame made for the Develop at Ubisoft Program. 
# Project Overview
#Controls

The game supports both **Keyboard & Mouse** and **Xbox Controllers**.

| Action            | Keyboard                | Xbox Controller            |
|-------------------|-------------------------|----------------------------|
| Movement          | WASD                    | Left Joystick              |
| Dash              | Left Shift              | B / Button East            |
| Jump              | Spacebar                | A / Button South           |
| Attack            | Right Click / Mouse 1   | RT (Right Trigger)         |
| Interact / Pick up| E                       | X / Button West            |

> **Combat**  
> Use the crosshair in the center of your screen to aim your camera and shoot at enemies.

---

#  Enemies & Mechanics

The world is inhabited by dangerous creatures. Learn their patterns to survive:

### Pulpitos (Octos)
- Patrol specific points.
- If you enter their field of vision, they will charge at you.
- Perform melee attacks once close.

### Picudos (Stars)
- Rotate in synchronized, random patterns.
- If you cross their path, they launch long-range projectile attacks.

---

# Progression & Gems

Defeated enemies may drop **Gems**.

Collect them to:

- Upgrade your current abilities.
- Unlock brand-new skills.

# Your Mission

Your ship has crashed, and its vital components are scattered across the planet.

Your objective:
- Find the **Ship Piece**.
- Repair your vessel.
- Return to your home planet.

# How to compile the project 
## 1️ Open in Unity

1. Open **Unity Hub**.
2. Click on **Add** → **Add project from disk**.
3. Select the cloned project folder.

> [!IMPORTANT]  
> This project requires **Unity version 2022.3.62f3**.  
> If you don't have it installed, download it via **Unity Hub** to avoid compatibility issues.

---

## 2️⃣ Build and Run

Once the project is loaded in the Unity Editor:

1. Go to **File** → **Build and Run**
2. The game will launch automatically after building.
